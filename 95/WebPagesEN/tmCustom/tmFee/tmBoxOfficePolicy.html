Box Office Policy

Children
Children under the age of six are not admitted to Festival plays. For children six and older, please familiarize yourself with the plays before ordering tickets. Information is available on our website at www.bard.org/plays.html. Child care for children up to the age of ten years is provided during all plays, but not during other Festival activities. Our professionally staffed, safe, and state-licensed facility is located at 43 S. 200 West Street. The cost is $15 for the first child and $10 for each additional sibling.

Exchanges

Tickets may be exchanged if new tickets are available and if the Ticket Office is given twenty-four hour notice. A $5 service fee will be charged for each ticket exchanged.

All plays, times, and prices are subject to change without notice.

All times are Mountain Time.
