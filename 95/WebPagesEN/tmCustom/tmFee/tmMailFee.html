Mailing Fees
Mailing fees are charged to cover the costs of sending you your tickets and/or passes in the mail. Tickets and Passes are mailed via 1st Class Postage. Delivery may take 5 to 7 business days to arrive.

It is important to note that no tickets will be mailed for performances within 7 days of the purchase date. Instead they will be held at Will Call on the day of the performance until 12 noon for matinees and until 6 p.m. for evening performances.


D_COMPANY_LONG is not responsible for lost or stolen tickets in the mail.
