Use this page to manage your Season Subscription Packages (to renew them or add new ones to your account). You are limited to the following:

  1. Renewing a package as is (without changes)
  2. Renewing your current seats and completing the online change request for a seat or performance move
  3. Cancelling a subscription to allow us to reallocate the seats later in the season
  4. Reinstating a package that you cancelled so you can book the seats
  5. Adding a completely new subscription after finding available seats

If you wish to make any wholesale changes to your subscription such as dropping or adding a seat -or- moving within the low(D_VENUE) or changing your evening that cannot be accomodated in the online change request, please contact the box office and we will be happy to assist.
